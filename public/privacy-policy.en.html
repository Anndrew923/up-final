Ultimate Physique Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

1. Scope

This policy explains how Ultimate Physique collects, uses, stores, and manages data while you use the app, including core assessments, leaderboard participation, and cloud-sync related functionality.

2. Data We May Process

  • Account data, such as Google sign-in identity (if you choose to sign in).
  • Profile data, such as display name, height, weight, age, sex, and training fields.
  • Assessment data, including scores, history snapshots, and calculation inputs.
  • Leaderboard summary fields required for ranking display.
  • Device-local data such as cache, preferences, and language settings.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide scoring, radar display, and history tracking features.
  • To support leaderboard and cloud-sync features when you enable them.
  • To maintain security, reliability, and abuse prevention controls.

4. Local-First and Cloud Sync

Ultimate Physique is designed with a local-first model: core features can function without mandatory cloud upload. Data is synced to cloud services only when related features are enabled.

5. Subscription, Billing, and Data Relationship

Pro features may include leaderboard access and cloud sync. Pricing and subscription terms may be updated over time and are disclosed in the product UI and/or store listing. Billing lifecycle details (including renewal, cancellation, and refunds) follow platform and store policies.

If billing models or platform rules change materially, this policy will be updated with a new revision date.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party infrastructure services (such as Firebase Authentication and database services) to provide sign-in, storage, and sync capabilities. Their terms and privacy policies may also apply.

7. Retention and Deletion

  • Local data is controlled by your device and may be unrecoverable once removed.
  • Cloud data retention depends on service requirements, account status, and legal obligations.
  • You may request account/cloud-data deletion through official support channels. We process requests in a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal and security retention requirements.

8. Your Rights

Where applicable by law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing. You may also withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy due to feature changes, legal obligations, or security governance updates. Material updates will be communicated through in-app notices or other reasonable channels.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the official support channel listed in the app settings.

11. Leaderboard Community Safety and User Content (UGC, Reporting, and Blocking)

To maintain a fair, secure, and healthy competitive environment within the Leaderboard (Arena), the following data processing and content moderation policies apply when you enable and use the leaderboard synchronization features:

  • Public Information Disclosure: When you synchronize your scores to the leaderboard, your display nickname, custom avatar, and six-axis athletic summary data will be publicly visible to other authenticated users. You must not use nicknames that contain sensitive personal data, hate speech, explicit, or offensive content.
  • Cloud-Based Reporting Records (Report): Any authenticated user may report content that violates our community standards. When you submit a report, the system securely logs the following on our servers: the reporter’s account identifier (UID), the target subject’s identifier (UID), the report type (nickname/avatar), and the timestamp. These records are used strictly for security, moderation, and legal compliance. All reporting records will be permanently deleted from our cloud database within 6 months after the case is resolved or closed.
  • Local Client Blocking (Block): We provide a personalized view-filtering mechanism. When you choose to block a user, that user’s identifier is stored strictly within your device’s local storage (LocalStorage) and is never uploaded to our cloud servers. This action only affects the visual display on your own device.
  • Content Moderation and Enforcement Rights: The service employs dual-gate automated profanity filtering on both the client and server sides. Reported content will be reviewed by administrators within a reasonable timeframe. We reserve the right, at our sole discretion and without prior notice, to sanitize nicknames, reset avatars, or suspend leaderboard access for accounts that violate community safety guidelines.
