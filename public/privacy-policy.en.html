UP Final Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-24

1. Scope

This policy explains how UP Final collects, uses, stores, and manages data while you use the app, including core assessments, leaderboard participation, and cloud-sync related functionality.

2. Data We May Process

  • Account data, such as Google sign-in identity (if you choose to sign in).
  • Profile data, such as display name, height, weight, age, sex, and training fields.
  • Assessment data, including scores, history snapshots, and calculation inputs.
  • Leaderboard summary fields required for ranking display.
  • Device-local data such as cache, preferences, and language settings.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide scoring, radar display, and history tracking features.
  • To support leaderboard and cloud-sync features when you enable them.
  • To maintain security, reliability, and abuse prevention controls.

4. Local-First and Cloud Sync

UP Final is designed with a local-first model: core features can function without mandatory cloud upload. Data is synced to cloud services only when related features are enabled.

5. Subscription, Billing, and Data Relationship

Pro features may include leaderboard access and cloud sync. Pricing and subscription terms may be updated over time and are disclosed in the product UI and/or store listing. Billing lifecycle details (including renewal, cancellation, and refunds) follow platform and store policies.

If billing models or platform rules change materially, this policy will be updated with a new revision date.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party infrastructure services (such as Firebase Authentication and database services) to provide sign-in, storage, and sync capabilities. Their terms and privacy policies may also apply.

7. Retention and Deletion

  • Local data is controlled by your device and may be unrecoverable once removed.
  • Cloud data retention depends on service requirements, account status, and legal obligations.
  • You may request account/cloud-data deletion through official support channels. We process requests in a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal and security retention requirements.

8. Your Rights

Where applicable by law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing. You may also withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy due to feature changes, legal obligations, or security governance updates. Material updates will be communicated through in-app notices or other reasonable channels.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the official support channel listed in the app settings.
